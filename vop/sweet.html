your smiling, weeping and human face,
which moves and lives unlike the stone in empty space,
for this at least is human, you i praise.

i sorrow for your loss, all beautiful things,
were meant to shine in the bright day.
yet life, most beautiful of all has left you today.

in my heart the treasure lies, tell me,
are there no divers in these parts who,
could pluck my wealth from this fathomless sea.

cursed be the moment, when we came too close,
o harsh fate, if you have my wealth.
alas ! my soul, it was cruel mercy then to leave.
take this beggard empty body and make it bare of all.

it is such a strange inhospitable terrain,
where the death's dice are yet to be thrown,
yer i breathe joy in the darkest sunlight.

neither am i week of hand nor feeble of heart,
i have harsher embrace and stronger will.
not aware that this terrain's ground forbade my unknown feet.

i am tired of hollow words, let my silence speak,
leave me to play with my deepest fears,
least it only comes to me like someone who i own.

i will wait till i meet a kinder mortal,
who will persuade me with a delightful smile,
to see the world full of beauty and style.

i hear someone lurking behind the flirtious hate,
oh again! it's you my loving fate.
